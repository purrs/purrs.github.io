Join our volunteer team!
Just a few hours per week makes all the difference.

There are many ways to volunteer with PURRS, requiring only as much time as you can offer. Check out our open positions, and when you are ready, fill out our Volunteer Application!

  • Cat Care Assistant

    About 2 hours per week

    Commit to one day per week to visit a pet store and care for our cats. You’ll give them fresh food, clean litter, and plenty of play time. You can choose the pet store and time of day that works best for you.

  • Photographer

    1–3 hours per week

    Do you have a digital camera and want to make your own hours?  We’re always looking for ways to capture our sweet cats.  Great pictures help get cats adopted!

  • Cat Transport

    4 hours per week (Wednesdays and/or Saturdays)

    Do you have a car, and the ability to lift cats in carriers?  We rotate cats between the three pet stores, plus trips to the vet.  Moves are carefully planned in order to be as efficient as possible.

  • Administrative/Office Helper

    2–3+ hours per week

    Flexible schedules, working from home on the phone and/or computer, requiring just a few hours per week.

  • Short-Term Foster Care

    One week or more based on need/availability

    Some kitties desperately need a break from the cage so they can relax, heal, etc. You don’t need much space at home to foster a cat or kitten for a few days!  A spare bathroom works fine, a spare bedroom or office is amazing. We’ll provide supplies if necessary.

  • Adoption Counselor

    3 hours on a Saturday or Sunday

    Help potential adopters meet the cats and decide if they’re ready to become pet owners. (Previous adoption/shelter experience preferred, or you can aspire to this position after getting to know PURRS through cat care.)

  • Adoption Event Helper

    3 hours on a Saturday or Sunday

    Weekend adoption events at the pet stores can get busy in a hurry. You’ll support the adoption counselor with various odd jobs, chat with prospective adopters, and of course, play with the cats.  This position is usually just a few hours on a weekend day, as needed.  An easy way to get your feet wet with volunteering!

If you’re interested, please fill out the Volunteer Application, and one of our schedulers will be in touch with more details about the position. There is no obligation! We would love to just speak to you. If you are at all interested, please do not hesitate to fill out the form and see if this can be a good fit!
