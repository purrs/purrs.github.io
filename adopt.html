Let’s find your new pet!

Check out the photos below to see all of our currently available cats. We have no shelter—the majority of our cats are housed at the Naperville Petco, Naperville Petsmart and Aurora Petsmart. You can visit them during regular store hours, or see them in action during our adoption hours on Saturdays and Sundays from 1-4pm.

Our adoption fees are: $110 for adult cats and $150 for kittens. If you adopt two cats, there is an additional discount! Purebreds are $50 extra. All cats are spayed/neutered, current on all age-appropriate shots, and microchipped. We accept cash, check, or charge.

Looking for a specific type of cat that isn’t listed? Just let us know, and we’ll get right to work finding you the perfect match!
