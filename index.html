PURRS Naperville is a non-profit, volunteer-run cat rescue serving the western suburbs of Chicago.

You can visit our cats in three local pet stores â€” Petco in Naperville, PetSmart in Aurora, and PetSmart in Naperville. Our volunteers are available during weekly adoption hours/events, and also by appointment.

Our expert staff and volunteers strive to find loving homes for stray or surrendered cats. We have years of experience to offer current pet owners and prospective adopters.

At PURRS, your adoption fees and donations go directly towards helping another cat get off the streets and into a warm home. Adopting one cat means you will actually save the life of two: the cat you took home, and a new cat, which now has a space at our shelter.

We welcome questions of any kind, whether about one of our adoptable cats, behavior advice, or inquiries about joining our team.
