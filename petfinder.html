Welcome to PURRS Naperville!
We are a non-profit, volunteer-run cat rescue serving the western suburbs of Chicago.

Featured Pet: Pickles

Pickles is nervous in a cage and desperately wants a loving home! She is fluffy and loves to cuddle. Her special price is just $75 to help her get her wish. Can you help?

PURRS Naperville is a 501(3)(c) charitable organization and the largest cat rescue in DuPage County. We have no physical shelter—the majority of our cats are housed at the Naperville Petco, Naperville Petsmart and Aurora Petsmart. You can visit the cats during regular pet store hours, see them in action during our adoption hours on Saturdays and Sundays from 1-4pm, or anytime by appointment!
